Pizza

Description

It's a pizza. Pretty straightforward. Cheese, no toppings, lets get on with it.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Whatever your heart desires

Steps

  1. Begin by making the pizza. This is accomplished by:
    1. Rolling the dough into a circle
    2. Putting the sauce, then cheese onto it. Leave space for the crust.
    3. Throw it into the wood-fired oven which is mandatory for this recipe
    4. Pizza made.
  2. Interlude. Be prepared with music to help it go by quicker.
  3. Completed!
